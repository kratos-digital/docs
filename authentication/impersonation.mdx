---
title: "User impersonation"
description: "Login as your customer from Admin panel."
---

<Info>Login as other Admin is not supported.</Info>

You are allowed to implement this feature if it's required for your application.

## How to impersonate

Login as Admin, go to Users, and under actions select `Login as user`.

Once you are logged in as your customer there will be banner notification where you can see the info of current impersonated user and/or stop the impersonation.
