Stripe provides invoices and receipts when using **subscriptions** from their own system at no additional charge.

For **single** orders, you should implement your own logic, setup advanced Stripe invoicing or use external services.

<Info>
  There will be feature to generate in-app invoices in **{appName}** **v2**.
</Info>
